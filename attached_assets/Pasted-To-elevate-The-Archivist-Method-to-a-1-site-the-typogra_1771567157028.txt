### Typography Implementation Tips for .1% Sites

* 
**The "3–7 Second" Highlight:** Use a Monospace font (like **JetBrains Mono**) for any numbers or biological timers to make them look like real-time data.


* **Text as Texture:** Don't be afraid to use **Large-Scale Typography** (e.g., `font-size: 15vw`) behind your interactive animations to create depth without slowing the site down.
* 
**Letter Spacing:** For your subheadlines (e.g., "The Science of Pattern Interruption"), use `letter-spacing: 0.2em` and `text-transform: uppercase` for a high-end fashion/tech look.
