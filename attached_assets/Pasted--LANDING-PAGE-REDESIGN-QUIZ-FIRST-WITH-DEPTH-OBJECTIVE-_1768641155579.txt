## COPY GUIDELINES

### Voice:
- **Direct, not academic:** "You watch yourself do it" not "Individuals often observe themselves engaging in..."
- **Brutally honest:** "You destroy things right before they succeed" not "You may experience challenges with completion"
- **Clinical but accessible:** "3-7 second window" (specific) not "brief moment" (vague)
- **No therapy jargon:** Never use "healing journey," "inner child," "self-love," etc.

### Formatting:
- **Short paragraphs:** 2-3 sentences max
- **White space:** Let content breathe
- **Bold sparingly:** Only for emphasis, not decoration
- **Lists with bullets:** When showing features/steps

## FINAL NOTES

### The Strategy:
This landing page follows a **quiz-first, depth-available** model:
- High-intent visitors see hero → click quiz (30-40% of traffic)
- Skeptics scroll, read patterns → recognize themselves → click quiz (40-50% of traffic)
- Deep researchers read everything → convinced by method → click quiz (10-15% of traffic)
- Remaining 5-10% bounce (acceptable loss)

### Success Metrics:
- **Primary:** Quiz start rate (target: 60-75% of visitors)
- **Secondary:** Scroll depth (target: 70% reach patterns section)
- **Tertiary:** Time on page (target: 2+ minutes for engaged users)

### Post-Launch:
After initial launch, A/B test:
1. Hero headline variations
2. Pattern card copy
3. CTA button copy ("Take Quiz" vs "Find My Pattern" vs "Start Free")
