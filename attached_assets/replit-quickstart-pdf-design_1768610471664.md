## FORMATTING DETAILS

### Markdown to PDF Conversion:
- `# Headers` → Large teal section headers (new page for major sections)
- `## Subheaders` → Medium teal headers
- `### Minor headers` → White/gray bold text
- `**Bold text**` → Keep as bold emphasis
- `---` dividers → Visual horizontal lines (teal or gray)
- Lists → Clean bullet points or numbered lists
- Indented quotes/scripts → Visually distinct (box, indent, or background)

### Text Hierarchy:
1. Major section headers (SECTION 1, etc.) - Largest, teal, new page
2. Week/phase headers (WEEK 1, etc.) - Large, teal
3. Subsections (Objective, The Work, etc.) - Medium, teal or white bold
4. Body text - Standard white/light gray, readable size
5. Scripts/examples - Indented or boxed, slightly smaller or italicized
