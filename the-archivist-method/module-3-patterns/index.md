## THE NINE

1. **The Disappearing Pattern.** Pulls away when intimacy increases.
2. **The Apology Loop.** Apologizes for existing, asking, needing.
3. **The Testing Pattern.** Creates secret tests to see if people really care.
4. **Attraction to Harm.** Mistakes danger for chemistry, familiar for safe.
5. **The Draining Bond.** Stays in what depletes them out of pattern, not loyalty.
6. **Compliment Deflection.** Reflex to reject, minimize, or redirect praise.
7. **The Perfectionism Trap.** Revises endlessly because done never feels safe.
8. **Success Sabotage.** Itch to blow things up when they start going well.
9. **The Rage Pattern.** Frustration bypasses thought and becomes explosion.

You run at least one. Probably two or three. One is primary.

Start there.
