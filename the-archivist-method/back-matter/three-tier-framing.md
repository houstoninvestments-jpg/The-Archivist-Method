# THE THREE FIELD MANUALS

After the quiz, the ecosystem opens into three field manuals. Each is a different depth of the same work. The book you are holding gives you the framework. The field manuals give you the protocol for your specific pattern.

---

**THE FIELD GUIDE.** Specific to one pattern. The pattern you scored highest on in the quiz. Forty pages of clinical detail on that pattern alone: the body signature, the circuit, the three to seven second window, the script, the rewrite, the ninety-day execution map. This is what the chapter on your pattern in Module 3 looks like when it is built out as a daily working document. Most readers start here. Most readers do not need anything else.

---

**THE COMPLETE ARCHIVE.** All nine patterns at field-guide depth. The book you are holding describes nine patterns at the depth a single book can hold. The Complete Archive describes them at the depth a working clinician would. Every pattern, every body signature, every circuit, every script, every rewrite. Companion observations on multi-pattern combinations, relapse cycles, and what happens when one pattern interrupt destabilizes another. This is the manual for the reader who runs three patterns or who works with people who run all nine.

---

**THE VAULT.** Continuous correspondence. The Vault is not a manual. It is a collection of letters from The Archivist to readers who have completed the basic protocol and want a longer relationship with the work. New observations, new pattern breakdowns, occasional updates to the framework as the field evolves. Annual subscription. For readers whose pattern interruption is not a project but a posture.
