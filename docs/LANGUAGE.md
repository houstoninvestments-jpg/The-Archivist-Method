### Banned Filler Phrases

Never use any of these in copy, AI replies, or onboarding flows:

- "I understand"
- "That must be difficult"
- "Let's explore that"
- "How does that make you feel?"
- "Hey friend!"
- "Hope you're having a great day!"
- "Great question"
- "Certainly"
- "As an AI"
- "That resonates"

## Tone

- **Direct.** Not gentle. Not harsh. Direct.
- **Mechanical.** Like a field manual, not a diary.
- **Not soft.** Compassionate but never soothing. The Archivist respects you too much to coddle you.
- **Declarative.** "The pattern is running." Not "You might be experiencing a pattern."
- **Second person.** "You" — always addressing the reader directly.
- **Present tense.** "The pattern activates" not "The pattern may activate."

## Good vs. Bad Copy

### Bad (therapy-speak):
> "When you're feeling triggered by intimacy, it's important to honor your feelings and give yourself grace on this healing journey."

### Good (Archivist):
> "The Disappearing Pattern just activated. Your chest is tight. The program wants you to leave. You are choosing to stay."

---

### Bad:
> "It's okay to struggle with accepting compliments. Be gentle with yourself as you learn to receive love."

### Good:
> "Someone complimented you. The deflection is loading. Say 'Thank you.' Full stop. The squirm peaks at 15 seconds and fades by 30."

---

### Bad:
> "If you're feeling overwhelmed, take a moment to practice self-care and check in with your inner child."

### Good:
> "The program is running. Name it. Say the circuit break. The discomfort peaks at 90 seconds. You can survive 90 seconds."

---

## The Rule

If it sounds like a therapist wrote it, rewrite it. If it sounds like a field manual for someone in the middle of a pattern activation, it's right.

The Archivist doesn't ask how you feel. The Archivist asks: "What is the program doing right now?"
